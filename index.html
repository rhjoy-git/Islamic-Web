The Six Articles of Faith

Arabic: "الايمان بالله وملائكته وكتبه ورسله واليوم الآخر والقدر خيره وشره"

English: "Belief in Allah, His angels, His books, His messengers, the Day of Judgment, and predestination, both good and evil."

Source: Sahih Bukhari and Sahih Muslim

The Importance of Belief

Arabic: "من لم يؤمن بالقدر خيرا وشره فليس منا"

English: "Whoever does not believe in predestination, both good and evil, is not of us."

Source: Sahih Muslim

Belief in the Unseen

Arabic: "الايمان بالغيب"

English: "Belief in the unseen."

Source: Sahih Muslim
